WHY?

WHY? is an indie rock/hip-hop band from Cincinatti, OH. They came to prominence with the debut of their first full-length studio album Elephant Eyelas. Fronted by lyricist and lead vocalist, Yoni Wolf, thye band have released four albums since 2005: Alopecia (2008), Eskimo Snow (2009), Mumps, Etc. (2012), and Moh Lhean (2017).

Current members include:

  • Yoni Wolf--vocals, synths, piano, samples, programming, bass guitar, and percussion.
  • Dough Mcdiarmid--keyboards, synths, piano, guitars, bass guitar, backing vocals.
  • Matt Meldon--guitars, bass guitar, keyboards, synths, backing vocals
  • Josiah Wolf--drums, percussion, keyboards, synths, samples, backing vocals
